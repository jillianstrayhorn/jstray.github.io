Lab Director

Jillian C. Strayhorn, PhD

Assistant Professor, NYU School of Global Public Health
Associate Director, CADIO

Jillian C. Strayhorn is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Social and Behavioral Sciences at the NYU School of Global Public Health. Her research advances intervention optimization research methods, with a particular focus on methods for multi-criteria decision-making in intervention development and optimization. She has led development of Decision Analysis for Intervention Value Efficiency (DAIVE), a framework for identifying high-value interventions under real-world resource constraints.
