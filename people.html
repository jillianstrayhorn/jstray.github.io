Jillian C. Strayhorn, PhD

Director, CADIO Lab

Jillian C. Strayhorn is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Social Behavioral Sciences at the NYU School of Global Public Health and Associate Director of CADIO. Her research advances intervention optimization research methods, with a particular focus on methods for multi-criteria decision-making in intervention optimization. She has led the development of Decision Analysis for Intervention Value Efficiency (DAIVE), a framework for identifying high-value interventions under real-world resource constraints.
